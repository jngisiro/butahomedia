Our Story

our history

Butaho Media Solution Ltd operates as a professional business committed to quality and exceeding our client’s expectations. Butaho Media Solution Ltd is an incorporated company limited by shares. We have built a strong reputation in the whole of Uganda by always putting the customer first and assisting them in achieving the most effective outcome with our wealth of experience.

Our mission

Our Mission is to surpass our client's expectations on every project with superior quality, service and value. We will strive to deliver individualised solutions to all our client's printing needs and add value to our clients' businesses in a timely manner.


Our vision

Our vision is to endeavour to become recognised as the benchmark of outstanding service, not just in the printing industry but across all industries. We pride ourselves on bringing results that count in everything that we set out to do and we are constantly striving for successful outcomes in all areas of our business.

Our goal

Our goal is to provide our clients with the highest quality printing, duplicating and graphic design services available today, in the fastest, most economical way possible. In doing so, we will create long-lasting, mutually beneficial relationships.
