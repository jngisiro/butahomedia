Offset Printing

Offset printing enables us to provide high quality print in large volumes. The finished product offers extremely high image quality that is clean, detailed and vibrant in colour.

Offering quick print turnaround times for large volume runs, offset printing also offers a large variety of paper coating and finishing options.

Benefits of offset printing:

  • On large quantities, offset printing is exceptionally cost effective. The time to set up the machinery prohibits it being competitive on short runs.
  • Printing spot colours using the PMS (Pantone Management System) enables us to accurately produce a specific colour should it be required as part of a business's branding.
  • Full colour printing allows us to produce high quality printed material on a range of different paper stocks.
  • Generic items can be printed in bulk and stored for when the customer needs them, cutting down on delivery times and making them cheaper over all.
  • Items printed in full colour offset can then be put back through a digital process for overprinting. This is particularly effective if producing large quantities of business cards. We can print a generic business card shell and then overprint each person's unique details as and when required.
