Promotional Products

Put your stamp on the world and promote your business in a unique way! Butaho Media Solutions Ltd offers more than just paper-based printing and can offer a variety of promotional products to help your business.

Promotional products offer a unique way to provide additional exposure of your brand to your clients and prospects. Creating an effective marketing strategy that includes the use of branded promotional items is very important. Even the smallest of products can really make an impact when promoting your company and provide the exposure needed to make your business a success.

First and foremost – make sure you are selecting the right product for your market. Giving promotional items to your clients and prospects are always well received and long remembered. They increase your brand visibility, help your clients and prospects remember your products and services, which then makes them more likely to buy from you. Take a closer look at our variety of promotional Items available to see what works best for you!

Calendars

Looking for a unique but useful promotional gift? Offering a customised calendar is a fantastic way to get exposure to your business year round.

They are a cost-effective marketing tool that can be completely customised and branded with your business information.

Pick your theme, showcase your products, use advertising space... it is an extremely useful way to create brand awareness!

CD Covers

Do you need a custom designed and printed CD cover? We can assist with the design and print, so it is consistent with your marketing material and makes a statement to your customers and prospects.

Seasonal greeting cards

Brand your Christmas Cards with your Business Logo and personalised message and make a wonderful statement about your business.

Whether you are looking at using an existing design from our catalogue of cards or would like customised artwork – we can certainly assist to make the best statement for your business this coming festive season!

Flyers & Brochures

Printing flyers and brochures are a convenient and cost effective way to share information about your business and promote key messages to your customers and prospects.

A great eye-catching design and a professional print job will make all the difference when you are advertising your key message using flyers. A4, DL (or more accurately 1/3 of an A4) and A5 Flyers are quite common, but we can create any size you need with a variety of paper stocks available.

Gift Bags

Personalised Gift Boxes are perfect for corporate gifting and can be custom designed to suit whatever you need them for!

Whether you need them branded with your business logo and information or are using for the festive season or thank you gifts, we can design and print the perfect gift bags for your needs.

Phone Cases

Butaho Media Solutions offer customised phone cases that can really showcase your business! Use your business logo, images, contact information – make it fun and unique and watch the compliments come in!

Business Cards

Business cards are an absolute essential item for your company and showcase your brand and values to your clients and prospects. It is an extension of your business and you need to make sure it showcases you in the best possible light.

The variety of cards available is endless in terms of card stock, design, shape and finish. Whether they are single or double sided, gloss or matt celloglazed, printed on uncoated specialty paper, die-cut, embossed, foiled, spot uv....the list is large and varied.

At the end of the day make sure your design is clean, professional and on good card stock as your business card will be the best networking tool you will ever have.
